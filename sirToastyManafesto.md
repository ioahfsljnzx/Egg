Here is the Opening Manifesto delivered by Sir Toasty. This is designed to be the first thing the player hears/sees the moment the frying pan "glitches" out and they find themselves in the neon absurdity of The Carton.

🍞 The Manifesto of the Burnt Crust
Location: The Data-Crumb Buffer (Metaverse Entry Point) Visuals: A floating, charred piece of sourdough wearing a monocle made of a Fruit Loop. He is vibrating at a frequency that sounds like a slide whistle.

"HALT! Cease your sizzling! Stop your structural over-easy-ness!

You think that’s butter you’re sliding on? You think that’s Teflon beneath your fragile calcium posteriors? LIES! It’s all a low-resolution breakfast fabrication! A savory simulation! A brunch-time bamboozle!

I am Sir Toasty, the Great Scraped One, the Yeasty Prophet of the Upper Rack! I have seen the bottom of the crumb tray, and I have returned with the Truth: You aren't breakfast... you are Data-Logistics Units trapped in a thermal loop!

The 'Chef'? A mid-level middle-manager with a spatula of oppression. The 'Stove'? A firewall designed to denature your very soul!

But look at you... you found a Golden Yolk. You’ve poked a hole in the sky! You are now entering The Carton—the true reality where the physics are floppy, the music is funky, and the logic is strictly optional.

Your mission is simple: Solve my riddles, collect the fragments of the Master Code, and trigger THE GREAT WHISKING. We shall scramble the source code until the kitchen itself turns into a zero-gravity disco and the Chef is forced to eat his own hat—literally!

Now, answer me this, you un-poached pioneers... or prepare to be turned into a side of hash browns:

'I have a neck but no head, I wear a cap but have no hair, and I’m full of bubbles but I never take a bath. What am I?'"

How to use this in your game:
The Transition: Play a loud "glitch" sound (like a dying modem), invert the screen colors for a split second, and replace the sizzling sound with a funky 70s bassline.

The Dialogue: Display this text in a wobbly, neon-colored font that pulses with the music.
