This tutorial triggers immediately after the player answers Sir Toasty’s first riddle. The screen shouldn't just "show" a mustache—it should feel like the game’s code is being rewritten by a hyperactive toddler.

🥸 Tutorial: Protocol "Stiff Upper Lip"
The Setting: Sir Toasty is vibrating so hard he’s shedding digital crumbs. The background is a kaleidoscope of dancing bacon strips wearing tutus.

The Meta-Instruction
Sir Toasty: "CORRECT! A Soda Bottle! Your yolk is dense with wisdom, young albumen! You have earned your first Logistical Glitch. We are going to bypass the Chef’s 'Omelet Detection Software' using ancient, forbidden facial hair geometry!"

How to Equip: The Mustache Matrix
The Manifestation: A giant, 8-bit handlebar mustache floats down from a neon cloud.

The Command: "To initiate the deception, you must wiggle your shell left-right-left (or Press [Shift] + [Q]). This confuses the kitchen's thermal sensors into thinking you are a Sophisticated Victorian Gentleman and not a breakfast item."

The Effect: A prompt appears on screen:

ERROR: [Egg_Model_01] has been overwritten by [Fancy_Lad_v4.2]

Tutorial Gameplay Prompt
The Goal: A holographic "Practice Chef" appears in the Metaverse. He is holding a spatula and looking very hungry.

The Action: The player must activate the Mustache Glitch.

The Result: The Practice Chef stops, drops his spatula, and starts clapping politely.

Sir Toasty’s Commentary: "Huzzah! Look at him! He can’t fry you now—it would be a breach of social etiquette! He thinks you’re here to discuss the price of tea in the colonies!"

The "Return to Pan" Transition
Once the player masters the wiggle, Sir Toasty shouts:
"BACK TO THE GREASE! Go forth and be dapper!"

The Metaverse shatters like glass.

The player is slammed back into the Frying Pan.

Visual Change: The egg now has a permanent, slightly-too-large mustache that physics-reacts (flops) when they jump.

Gameplay Change: When the Chef moves to "flip" the player, the Mustache emits a Golden Aura. The Chef pauses for 2 seconds to tip his hat, giving the player a window to escape to the next burner.
